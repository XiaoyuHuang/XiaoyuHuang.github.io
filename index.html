Star Fall Racing

A space car racing game — built entirely from a single prompt by my 6-year-old daughter.

The story behind this
My daughter came up with the idea and described it to Claude AI exactly as a 6-year-old would. I wanted to test the aha moment — to see how AI interprets the imagination and intent of a child's prompt, and what it builds.
"design a game called star fall. there are cars that race. you can choose your color of your car. then you push a button space, then the car wil go go. when you cross the finish line it will tell you what is your ranking. you could also choose the number of your car."
Claude understood the vision — typos and all — and turned it into a fully playable game. The result speaks for itself.
